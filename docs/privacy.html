Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 28, 2026

Doction ("the App") is an open-source desktop application developed by Mehmet Emin Tok ("we", "us"). This policy describes how Doction handles your data.

1. What Data We Access

Doction connects to your Google account via OAuth 2.0 and requests the following read-only permissions:

  • drive.readonly — to list and read files and folders in your Google Drive
  • documents.readonly — to display the content of Google Docs
  • spreadsheets.readonly — to display the content of Google Sheets
  • openid, email, profile — to identify your account for authentication

Doction does not request write access. It cannot create, modify, or delete any files in your Google Drive.

2. How Your Data Is Processed

All data retrieved from Google APIs is processed locally on your device. Doction does not transmit your Google Drive content to any external server. The app communicates exclusively with Google's APIs to fetch the data you choose to view.

3. Data Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device:

  • OAuth tokens — stored securely in the Tauri application store to maintain your login session
  • User preferences — sidebar state, theme selection, favorites, and recently viewed files are stored in your browser's localStorage

No data is stored on remote servers operated by us. We do not operate any backend infrastructure.

4. Third-Party Services

Doction communicates with the following Google API services:

  • Google OAuth 2.0 (accounts.google.com) — for authentication
  • Google Drive API (www.googleapis.com/drive) — for file listing and metadata
  • Google Docs API (docs.googleapis.com) — for document content
  • Google Sheets API (sheets.googleapis.com) — for spreadsheet content

Your use of these services is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, or transfer your data to any third party. Since all processing happens on your device, we never have access to your data in the first place.

6. Data Retention & Deletion

To remove all data stored by Doction:

  • Log out within the app (this clears OAuth tokens)
  • Uninstall the application (this removes all local data)
  • Optionally, revoke Doction's access in your Google Account permissions

7. Children's Privacy

Doction is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. The source of this policy is maintained in our GitHub repository.

9. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository or contact us at the email listed in our GitHub profile.
