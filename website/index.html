Your Google Drive,
reimagined.

Doction is a desktop workspace that brings your Google Docs, Sheets, images and PDFs together in a clean, Notion-like interface.

Download for macOS

Everything in one place

📄

Google Docs Viewer

Read your documents with beautiful formatting — headings, lists, tables, and inline images rendered natively.

📊

Sheets & Spreadsheets

Browse your spreadsheet data in a clean table view without the overhead of a full browser tab.

📂

Folder Navigation

Navigate your Drive structure with a sidebar, breadcrumbs, and split-pane views — just like a native file manager.

🔍

Command Palette

Find any file instantly with a keyboard-driven command palette. No more digging through nested folders.

🎨

Images & PDFs

Preview images and PDF files directly in the app, with zoom and multi-page support.

⭐

Favorites & Recents

Pin your most-used files for quick access and pick up right where you left off with recent files.

Your data stays yours

Doction only requests read-only access to your Google Drive. No data is stored on external servers — everything stays on your device.
